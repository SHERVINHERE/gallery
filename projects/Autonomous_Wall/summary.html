Overview

What if a wall had legs and could walk? What if it had a brain of its own and could decide where to go? Where would it go? What would it like to do?

We say the lonely wall would love to join crowds — see what they are doing, and help them in any way it can. The friendly wall down the neighborhood finds crowds and provides them with privacy, a whiteboard, a presentation board, and more.
