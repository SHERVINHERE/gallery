Overview

The Adjustable Gum Retractor is a dental surgical device developed to provide stable and adjustable gum tissue retraction during gum surgery. It is designed to free both of the clinician’s hands by holding the gum tissue in a fixed position without the need for continuous manual assistance.

The device includes a face-conforming support frame that anchors securely inside the patient’s mouth and a movable retraction blade assembly that can be positioned along a circular track to access various areas of the gums. Its modular, mechanically simple design ensures flexibility for the surgeon, comfort for the patient, and improved surgical precision.
